Guardian's Garden

My first Unreal Engine game, inspired by Sorceress's Garden from Runescape. Multiplayer game where players get to run through a little maze and collect gems, the garden is guardian by patrol NPC's and they are ready to blast out any player in sight. This is a game of patience, timing and readiness.
