Abdulakh Kurbanov

Hello, I'm 29 y/o game-programmer student at Forsberg's School.

I have been into sales and service past 10 years but after deep and long consideration I decided to change career path and game programming was a good place to start since gaming, sci-fi and fantasy stories are my hobby so I thought what's better than learning more about something you enjoy and make a living of it.

I'm not entirely sure yet but I think my favorite things are Transformations and Shaders probably because Math is not my strongest suit and these areas offers most challenge and growth opportunity for me.

Since joining Forsberg I've learned to work in object oriented manner, creating custom classes, functions and optimization patterns, I also learned little about different algorithms and their use cases.

Probably most important thing I gained was general understanding of how teams work together to create something great, and that perfection is in the details.

When it comes to coding, I just follow the curious impulse led by 'What if and How' questions(not very efficent, time-wise but so far so good) and I often find myself hardstuck with bugs. So I think bug-tracing and hunting should be a class of it's own in games.

Personality & Social behaviors:

I'm usually calm and quiet person but can be light-hearted and playful sometimes.
